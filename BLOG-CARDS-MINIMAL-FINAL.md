## ✨ **What's Shown:**

✅ **Image** (220px tall, prominent)

## 🚫 **What's Hidden:**

❌ Category badge  
❌ Excerpt text

❌ "Read" link

## 🎨 **Design:**

**Card Shape:**
- Square aspect ratio (1:1.15)
- Min height: 340px
- Balanced proportions

**Image:**
- 220px tall
- Full width
- Visual focus

**Title:**
- Centered text
- 1.125rem font
- Bold weight
- 2 lines maximum
- Clean, minimal

**Content Padding:**
- 1.25rem all around
- Title vertically centered
- Professional spacing

---

## 📱 **Responsive:**

**Desktop:** 3 square cards per row  
**Tablet:** 2 square cards per row  
**Mobile:** 1 card per row, natural height

**Ultra Minimal Approach:**
- ✅ Image tells the story

- ✅ No clutter or distraction
- ✅ Modern, premium feel
- ✅ Instagram/Pinterest aesthetic

**User Experience:**
- ✅ Fast visual scanning
