What Happens on the Call

A 30-minute conversation to map your business and show you exactly what we'd build

1. Learn About Your Business (5 min)

  • • What industry are you in?
  • • How many leads per month?
  • • Current follow-up process?
  • • Biggest operational pain points?

2. Map Your Current Process (10 min)

  • • Where do leads come from?
  • • How are they handled?
  • • What's working? What's broken?
  • • Where are leads falling through?

3. Show You the System (10 min)

  • • Exactly what we'd build for you
  • • How automation would handle leads
  • • What your dashboard would look like
  • • Expected ROI and time savings

4. Build a Plan (5 min)

  • • Pricing tailored to your business
  • • Implementation timeline (2-4 weeks)
  • • Next steps if you want to move forward

No pressure. No hard sell. Just clarity.
