When I said "add a contact form," it didn't just add a form. It:

I didn't build this site by telling AI to "make me a website" and walking away. I built it through dozens of back-and-forth conversations:

*"The phone number needs to be clickable."*

I stayed in control. I made the decisions. The AI executed flawlessly and asked smart questions when something wasn't clear.
