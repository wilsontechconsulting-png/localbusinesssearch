## How We Help

### 1. Missed Call Recovery (COO Function)
Out on a job when someone calls? AI sends instant SMS: "Hey! We saw you called. Here's a link to book your appointment."

**Result:** Capture leads even when you're busy.

### 2. Instant Follow-Up (CMO Function)
Lead fills out a form → AI responds in < 60 seconds → Automated nurture if they're not ready yet.

**Result:** You're the first to respond. You get the job.

### 3. Automated Booking (COO Function)
No more phone tag. Customers book directly into your calendar. Auto-reminders reduce no-shows by 40%.

**Result:** Your schedule stays full. Less wasted time.

### 4. ROI Tracking (CFO Function)
Know exactly which ads → which leads → which revenue. Stop guessing where to spend your marketing budget.

**Result:** Spend more on what works. Cut what doesn't.

### 5. Dashboard Visibility (CEO Function)
See your entire operation in one place: leads, bookings, revenue, ad performance.

**Result:** Run your business with data, not gut feel.
