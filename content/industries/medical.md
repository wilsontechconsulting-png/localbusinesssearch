## How We Help

### 1. Instant New Patient Response (CMO Function)
Someone fills out your form or calls → AI responds instantly with appointment booking link.

**Result:** Book patients before they call your competitor.

### 2. Automated Appointment Booking (COO Function)
Patients book directly into your schedule. No staff time wasted on phone tag.

**Result:** Your front desk focuses on in-office patients, not scheduling.

### 3. No-Show Reduction (COO Function)
Auto-reminders via SMS + email 48 hours and 24 hours before appointment.

**Result:** Reduce no-shows by 40%. Protect your schedule and revenue.

### 4. Case Acceptance Follow-Up (CMO Function)
Patient didn't book their treatment plan? Automated nurture sequence brings them back.

**Result:** Increase case acceptance without pushy sales.

### 5. Patient Reactivation (CMO Function)
Haven't seen a patient in 6+ months? AI sends re-engagement campaign: "Time for your checkup."

**Result:** Bring back inactive patients automatically.

### 6. ROI & LTV Tracking (CFO Function)
Know which marketing channels bring in high-value patients. Track lifetime value by acquisition source.

**Result:** Invest in marketing that actually grows your practice.
