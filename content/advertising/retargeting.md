# Retargeting Campaigns

**Bring Back the Ones Who Got Away**

Strategic remarketing that turns website visitors into customers.

## Why Retargeting Works

**Only 2-3% of website visitors convert on their first visit.**

The other 97%? They leave.

Retargeting brings them back by:
- Reminding them of your services
- Showing social proof and offers
- Staying top-of-mind until they're ready

## How We Do It

### Multi-Channel Remarketing
- **Facebook/Instagram**: Visual ads to past visitors
- **Google Display**: Banners across millions of websites
- **YouTube**: Video ads to engaged visitors
- **Search Retargeting**: Show up when they search later

### Audience Segmentation
- Visited specific service pages
- Abandoned forms
- Spent 2+ minutes on site
- Past customers (reactivation)

### Sequential Messaging
Day 1: Remind them what you offer  
Day 3: Share a testimonial or case study  
Day 7: Special offer or urgency

## Connected to Your System

Retargeting ad → Landing page → AI capture → Automated follow-up → Conversion

Higher conversion rates. Lower cost per lead. Revenue tracked end-to-end.
