## 🚀 5-MINUTE SETUP

### 1. Get Your Free Access Key

Go to: **https://web3forms.com**
- Enter: `jed@powerofadvertising.com`
- Click "Create Access Key"
- Check your email
- Copy the access key

### 2. Add Key to Form

Edit: `/themes/lbs/layouts/section/contact.html`

Find this line (around line 27):
```html
<input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE">
```

Replace `YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE` with your actual key.

Save the file.

### 3. Update Redirect URL

Find this line (around line 30):
```html
<input type="hidden" name="redirect" value="https://yourdomain.com/contact/success/">
```

Replace with your production domain when you deploy.

For local testing: `http://localhost:1314/contact/success/`

### 4. Test!

Go to: http://localhost:1314/contact/
Fill out the form → Submit → Check your email!
