## 🧠 CEO/CFO/CMO/COO FRAMEWORK

This framework is now integrated throughout the site:

### CEO (Strategy & Visibility)
- Needs: Dashboard, real-time data, decision-making tools
- Gets: Full operational visibility, data-driven insights

### CFO (Financial Efficiency)
- Needs: ROI tracking, cost allocation, financial clarity
- Gets: Ad spend → lead → revenue tracking, LTV metrics

### CMO (Growth Systems)
- Needs: Advertising, conversion optimization, attribution
- Gets: Strategic campaigns connected to automation systems

### COO (Operations & Execution)
- Needs: Lead handling, appointment booking, process automation
- Gets: Efficient operations, reduced manual work, time savings

**Messaging:** "We connect all four. One system. Not four disconnected tools."
