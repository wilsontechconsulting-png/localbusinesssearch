Business Systems for Local Retail

Automate customer retention, increase foot traffic, and maximize lifetime value. Built for restaurants, coffee shops, boutiques, salons, and local retail businesses.

Automate customer retention, increase foot traffic, and maximize lifetime value. Built for restaurants, coffee shops, boutiques, salons, and local retail businesses.

The Problem

You get customers in the door. But they don’t come back:

  • One-time customers (no repeat business)
  • No follow-up system (they forget about you)
  • Manual loyalty tracking (punch cards get lost)
  • Can’t measure what brings people in
  • Slow seasons kill cash flow

You need systems that keep customers coming back.

How We Help

1. Automated Win-Back Campaigns (CMO Function)

Customer hasn’t visited in 30/60/90 days → AI sends personalized offer: “We miss you! Here’s 15% off your next visit.”

Result: Bring back inactive customers automatically.

2. Post-Visit Follow-Up (COO Function)

Customer visits → AI sends thank you + asks for review + offers loyalty program signup.

Result: Build relationships, get reviews, increase retention.

3. Loyalty Automation (COO Function)

Digital loyalty program (no punch cards). Track visits, reward automatically, send birthday offers.

Result: Increase customer lifetime value 2-3x.

4. Event & Promotion Campaigns (CMO Function)

New menu item? Seasonal sale? AI sends targeted campaigns to your customer base.

Result: Drive foot traffic when you need it.

5. ROI Tracking (CFO Function)

Know which marketing drives foot traffic. Track customer acquisition cost vs lifetime value.

Result: Invest in channels that bring in profitable customers.

6. Reservation & Booking (COO Function - Restaurants/Salons)

Online booking for tables, appointments, classes. Auto-reminders reduce no-shows.

Result: Fill your schedule, reduce wasted capacity.

Before vs After: Coffee Shop

Before

  • 400 customers/month
  • 15% return within 30 days (60 customers)
  • No follow-up or retention system
  • Average customer visits 1.2x/month

After (with our systems)

  • 400 customers/month
  • Automated win-back campaigns
  • Loyalty program enrollment: 65%
  • 40% return within 30 days (160 customers)
  • Average customer visits 2.8x/month

Result: +130% repeat business, higher per-customer revenue
