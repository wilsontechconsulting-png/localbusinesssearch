Business Systems for Law Firms

Automate case intake, qualify leads instantly, and track ROI by practice area. Built for personal injury, family law, estate planning, and legal practices.

Automate case intake, qualify leads instantly, and track ROI by practice area. Built for personal injury, family law, estate planning, and legal practices.

The Problem

You’re a great attorney. But your intake process is costing you cases:

  • Lead response is slow (hours or days)
  • Case qualification is manual (wasted time on unqualified leads)
  • Intake forms are paper-based (inefficient, error-prone)
  • Can’t track which marketing brings in profitable cases
  • Retainer collection is inconsistent

By the time you call back, they’ve already signed with someone else.

How We Help

1. Instant Lead Response (CMO Function)

Someone fills out your form → AI responds in < 60 seconds → Schedules intake consultation automatically.

Result: You’re the first firm to respond. You get the case.

2. AI Case Qualification (COO Function)

AI asks screening questions (practice area, case type, timeline) → Routes high-value cases to attorneys → Nurtures low-priority leads.

Result: Attorneys focus on cases worth their time.

3. Automated Intake (COO Function)

Digital intake forms pre-populate your case management system. No manual data entry.

Result: Save 2-3 hours/week per paralegal.

4. Consultation Booking (COO Function)

Qualified leads book directly into attorney calendars. No back-and-forth with reception.

Result: Fill your consultation slots automatically.

5. Retainer Tracking & Collection (CFO Function)

Automated retainer payment reminders. Track signed vs unsigned cases. Know your conversion rate.

Result: Increase signed case rate by 20-30%.

6. ROI by Practice Area (CFO Function)

Know which marketing channels bring in personal injury vs family law vs estate planning cases. Track case value and profitability.

Result: Invest in marketing that brings in profitable cases.
