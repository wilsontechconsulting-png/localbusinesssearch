The Problem

Your city has important information to share. But residents aren’t seeing it:

  • Public service messages get lost in the noise
  • Event attendance is low (town halls, festivals, meetings)
  • Resident engagement is poor (nobody reads the newsletter)
  • Can’t measure what’s working
  • Limited budget for communication

You need modern systems that actually reach people.

How We Help

  • Parks and recreation programs

Result: Reach residents where they already spend time (Facebook, Google).

2. Event Promotion (CMO Function)

Promote town halls, community festivals, public meetings, and city events with targeted digital campaigns.

3. Resident Engagement (COO Function)

Automated systems for:

Result: Better engagement, less manual work.

4. Economic Development (CMO Function)

Attract businesses and tourism with targeted campaigns highlighting your city’s strengths.

Result: Grow your tax base and economy.

5. Performance Tracking (CFO Function)

Know which campaigns work. Track reach, engagement, and cost per resident reached.

Rapid digital messaging for weather alerts, road closures, public safety notifications.

Result: Reach residents instantly when it matters most.

Before vs After: Town of 45,000 Residents

Before

  • Town hall meetings: 80 attendees (0.18% of population)
  • Summer festival marketing: Facebook posts + flyers
  • Attendance: 1,200 people
  • No way to track effectiveness

After (with our digital systems)

  • Town hall promotion: Targeted Facebook + Google ads to registered voters
  • Summer festival: Multi-channel digital campaign
  • Attendance: Town hall 240 (+200%), Festival 3,800 (+216%)
  • Full tracking and measurement

Result: 3x event attendance, measurable engagement
