TV-Quality Advertising. Digital Precision.

Reach local customers on streaming TV, connected devices, and premium apps—with targeting that traditional TV can’t match.

What Is Programmatic OTT/CTV?

Programmatic advertising means smart, automated ad buying.

Over-The-Top (OTT): Streaming apps (Hulu, Roku, etc.)
Connected TV (CTV): Smart TVs and streaming devices

Why It Works for Local Businesses

  • TV-quality creative (big brand feel)
  • Digital targeting (age, location, interests, behavior)
  • Measurable results (unlike traditional TV)
  • Affordable for local budgets

Use Cases

Home Services: Reach homeowners in your service area watching home improvement shows

Medical: Target health-conscious viewers 35-65 in your zip codes

Legal: Precision targeting for personal injury, family law, etc.

Retail: Drive foot traffic with geo-targeted CTV ads

Franchises: Multi-location campaigns with location-specific creative

Municipalities: Reach residents with public service messaging
