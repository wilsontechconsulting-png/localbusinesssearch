## 📝 How to Add Blog Posts

### **Step 1: Create a New Blog Post File**

Create a new file in: `/content/blog/your-post-slug.md`

Example: `/content/blog/5-ways-ai-automates-lead-response.md`

### **Step 2: Add Frontmatter**

```yaml
---
title: "5 Ways AI Automates Lead Response in Local Businesses"
date: 2026-04-23
author: "Jed Wilson"
category: "AI Automation"
tags: ["AI", "Lead Response", "Automation", "Business Growth"]
image: "/images/blog/ai-lead-response.jpg"
description: "Discover how AI can respond to leads instantly, qualify them automatically, and book appointments without human intervention."
---
```

### **Step 3: Write Content (Markdown)**

```markdown
## Introduction

Local businesses lose thousands of leads every year due to slow response times. Studies show that responding within 5 minutes increases conversion rates by 400%.

But what if you could respond **instantly**, 24/7?

## 1. Instant Lead Capture

When a lead fills out your form, AI can:
- Send immediate confirmation email
- Text the lead within seconds
- Add them to your CRM automatically

## 2. Lead Qualification

AI asks qualifying questions:
- Budget range
- Timeline to purchase
- Specific needs

Only qualified leads reach your sales team.

## 3. Appointment Booking

AI can:
- Check your calendar availability
- Offer time slots to the lead
- Book confirmed appointments
- Send reminders

## Conclusion

AI lead response isn't the future—it's here now. And it's affordable for local businesses.

Ready to implement AI in your business? [Schedule a demo](/demo/) to see it in action.
```

### **Step 4: Save & Reload**

The blog post will appear automatically!

## 🎨 Supported Frontmatter Fields

### **Required:**
- `title` - Post title (shown everywhere)
- `date` - Publication date (YYYY-MM-DD format)

### **Optional:**
- `author` - Author name (e.g., "Jed Wilson")
- `category` - Single category (e.g., "AI Automation", "Business Systems")
- `tags` - Array of tags (e.g., ["AI", "Automation", "Growth"])
- `image` - Featured image URL or path
- `description` - Meta description / summary (160 chars recommended)
